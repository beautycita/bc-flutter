## Style Guide (apply to ALL prompts)

**Consistent style directive (prepend to every prompt):**
> Elegant flat illustration, minimal line art with soft gradients, beauty/salon aesthetic. Deep rose (#660033) and warm gold (#FFB300) as primary accent colors on a clean white or cream (#FFF8F0) background. Sophisticated, feminine but inclusive, modern. No text or lettering in the image. Transparent or solid white background. Output as PNG with transparency where possible. 512x512px.

**For dark theme variants, swap the directive to:**
> Same illustration but on a dark background (#0A0A0F) with gold (#FFB300) and soft rose (#FF6B9D) accents.
