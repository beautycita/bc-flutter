02 — Solution

AI booking agent. Not another app.

BeautyCita is not a booking app — it is an intelligent booking agent. The customer selects a service type, the engine returns the top 3 best options with a pre-selected time slot, and the customer books with one tap. 4-6 taps, under 30 seconds, zero keyboard input.

For the salon, BeautyCita replaces 5+ tools for free: CRM, marketing, payments, appointment management, and tax compliance. BeautyCita earns 3% per transaction — no monthly fees, no setup costs, no contracts.

  • Intelligent service-type-aware booking engine (200-400ms response)
  • Time inference — no calendar pickers, no time grids
  • Built-in ISR/IVA withholding with SAT real-time API
  • Stripe Connect payments + cash registration
  • WhatsApp-first salon onboarding (60 seconds)
  • Free for salons — always. BC earns on transactions.
