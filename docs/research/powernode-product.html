The Vision
One system. Every device. Everywhere.
📱

One Screen, All Devices, All Platforms

Alexa, Google, Tuya, eWeLink, EZVIZ, Sciener, Broadlink, Zigbee, Matter — all controlled from a single dashboard. Switch between your house, your store, and your beach condo with one tap. No more app juggling.

⚡

Your Wiring IS Your Network

PowerNode uses powerline communication (PLC) to turn your existing electrical wiring into a high-speed data backbone. No new cables. No drilling. Plug a node into any outlet and it joins the network instantly — WiFi 6 and Gigabit Ethernet everywhere.

🌍

Take Your Network Anywhere

Unplug a node and take it to a hotel, an office, or a friend's house. Plug it in — it auto-authenticates with your home hub via encrypted VPN. You're on your home network. Same security. Same access. Your cameras, NAS, smart devices — all reachable.

🖥

Built-in Remote Desktop (PowerDesk)

Access any PC or Mac on your home network from anywhere — through your node's encrypted tunnel. Your hub IS the relay server. No monthly fees. No third-party servers. No setup. Just open the app and you're sitting at your desk.

How It Works
Four steps. Done.
From box to fully connected home in under an hour.
1

Install the Hub

DIN-rail mount at your breaker panel. Connect to your ISP. Power on.

2

Auto-Discovery

The hub finds ALL your smart devices across ALL platforms automatically. Alexa, Google, Tuya, Zigbee, Matter — all of them.

3

Extend with Nodes

Plug satellites into any outlet. They join the network over your wiring — instant WiFi 6 + Ethernet everywhere. Up to 64 per hub.

4

Take It Anywhere

Unplug a node and go. Hotel, office, friend's house — plug in and your entire home network is with you. Encrypted. Automatic.
